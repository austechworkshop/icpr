Keynote Speaker: Prof. Santanu Chaudhury

Title: Computer Vision: An Enabler for Blue Economy.

Abstract: Computer vision is a cornerstone technology for enabling and enhancing various aspects of the Blue Economy. By giving "eyes" to underwater and maritime systems, it provides critical data and automation capabilities across numerous sectors.

Biography: Prof. Santanu Chaudhury did his B.Tech (1984) in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering and Ph.D. (1989) in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Kharagpur, India. He is a professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Delhi, former Director of IIT Jodhpur and CSIR Central Electronics Research Institute, and a fellow of INAE, NASI, and IAPR. He has over 350 publications and 15 patents. His interests include Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, Digital Heritage, AR VR, and multi sensory media.
